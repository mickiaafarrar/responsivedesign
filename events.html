Want Our Treats For Your Event?

Looking for a venue to host a small event? BMC Bakes is the perfect setting for intimate celebrations, offering a fun and inviting atmosphere for showers, rehearsal dinners, and other small events. Whatever you are planning, our space accommodates 30 guests comfortably and is equipped with a full kitchen, an exposed staircase ideal for speeches, a cozy library loft, serving counters and includes tables and chairs. Let us host your next unforgettable event!

BMC Bakes is open 8am-6pm Wednesday through Saturday and 11am-5pm on Sunday. If you would like to host an event during this time, we are able to close the store for your private event.
