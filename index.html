About Us

BMC Bakes was established in 2022, originating from our founder's apartment kitchen. Initially operating as a home-based bakery, we built a dedicated customer base that highlighted the need for allergy friendly options in our area.

This led us to transition to the Harrisonburg Farmer's Market to further expand our reach. After a successful year at the market and growing our incredible community, we recognized the need for a dedicated gluten free storefront to accommodate increasing demand.

We are now proudly operating from our own location, continuing to serve our community with the same passion and quality that fueled our founder in her home kitchen.
